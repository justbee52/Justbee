Our Golden Goodness

Why Choose Raw, Unpasteurized Honey?

Our honey is raw and unpasteurized, meaning we preserve all of nature's beneficial enzymes, antioxidants, and nutrients that are typically destroyed during commercial heat processing. This commitment to maintaining honey in its natural state means you receive the maximum health benefits that our bees intended.

Raw honey may naturally crystallize over time â€“ this is actually a sign of quality and purity! Crystallization occurs more readily in raw honey because the natural sugars and particles that commercial processing removes are still present.

The beauty of honey: it never expires. Your JustBee honey will remain perfectly good forever when stored properly. If crystallization occurs, simply place the jar in a warm water bath and gently heat until it returns to its liquid golden state.
